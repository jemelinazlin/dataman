This report presents a summary of my attachment experience at Jamhuri Primary School, where I worked as a teacher during the attachment period. The main purpose of the attachment was to gain practical experience, apply theoretical knowledge acquired in class, and develop professional and interpersonal skills in a real working environment. I chose Jamhuri Primary School because of its accessibility and its reputation as a structured learning institution within the community.

The application for the attachment was done by submitting a formal request to the school administration, after which I was accepted and assigned to work under the supervision of experienced teaching staff. During the attachment period, I worked under agreed conditions which included a regular school schedule from Monday to Friday during official school hours. Although the attachment was not salaried, it provided valuable hands-on experience. My roles and responsibilities included preparing lesson plans, teaching learners, marking assignments, managing classroom activities, and assisting students with their academic work. Throughout the attachment, I was able to interact with both teachers and students, which helped me improve my communication, time management, and organizational skills. I also gained experience in classroom management and learned how to handle different student behaviors effectively. Overall, the attachment was a valuable experience that allowed me to bridge the gap between theory and practice. It helped me understand the dynamics of a real working environment and contributed significantly to my personal and professional growth
